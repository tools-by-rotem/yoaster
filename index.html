Welcome to Yoaster!

Your friendly URL decoder for Yoast Sitemaps

Transform Your Sitemap Experience

Ever found yourself squinting at those long, encoded URLs in Yoast's XML Sitemaps? Say goodbye to the guesswork with Yoaster! This handy Chrome extension instantly decodes URLs directly in the browser, turning every cryptic character into a readable, friendly link. Designed to work like magic on pages titled "XML Sitemap", Yoaster transforms the experience, making it clear, intuitive, and... just a little bit fun.
