Yoaster

Decode URLs in Yoast Sitemaps, Instantly!

Ever found yourself squinting at those long, encoded URLs in Yoast XML Sitemaps? Say goodbye to the guesswork with Yoaster! This handy Chrome extension instantly decodes URLs directly in the browser, transforming every cryptic character into a readable, friendly link. Designed to work seamlessly on pages titled "XML Sitemap," Yoaster makes navigating sitemaps clearer, more intuitive, and... just a little bit fun.

Features

  • Instant decoding of URLs in Yoast sitemaps
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
  • Seamless integration into your browser experience
  • Works on XML Sitemap pages
  • Fun and intuitive design

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I install Yoaster?

A: You can install Yoaster directly from the Chrome Web Store. Just search for "Yoaster" and click "Add to Chrome."

Q: Is Yoaster free to use?

A: Yes, Yoaster is completely free to use!

Q: Which browsers are compatible with Yoaster?

A: Yoaster is designed specifically for Google Chrome and is not compatible with other browsers at this time.

Q: What if I have issues with the extension?

A: If you encounter any problems, please contact our support team via the Chrome Web Store page.
